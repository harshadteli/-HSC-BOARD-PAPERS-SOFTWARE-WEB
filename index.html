📌 About This Software

This web-based application provides HSC (Higher Secondary Certificate) Board Exam Previous Year Question Papers for Science Students of Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE). It is designed to help students practice with authentic board papers.
